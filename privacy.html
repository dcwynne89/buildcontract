Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

When you use BuildContract without signing in, we do not collect or store any personal data. All contract data stays in your browser and is sent directly to the PDF generation endpoint. We do not store submitted contract data on our servers — PDFs are generated on the fly and returned immediately.

How We Use Your Data

Data you enter into the contract form is used exclusively to generate your PDF contract. We do not sell, share, or analyze your contract data. API usage data (request counts, timestamps) may be logged for rate-limiting and abuse prevention.

Optional Account & Data Storage

If you choose to sign in (via Google), your account is managed through Firebase Authentication. When signed in, you can save contracts to your account. Saved contract data is stored in Firebase Firestore and is only accessible by you. You can delete saved contracts at any time. Deleting your account removes all stored data.

Third-Party Services

BuildContract uses the following third-party services:

  • Netlify — Hosting and serverless functions
  • Google AdSense — Advertising (may set cookies for ad personalization)
  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and data storage (optional, only if you sign in)

Cookies

BuildContract itself does not set cookies. Third-party services (AdSense, Firebase) may set cookies as described in their respective privacy policies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at buildpdf.help@gmail.com.
